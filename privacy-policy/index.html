Rossler Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Rossler provides content operations, publishing, and analytics tools for authorized brand and business accounts.

Information We Collect

When users authorize Rossler to connect with third-party services such as Pinterest, we may collect limited information necessary to operate the service, including:

  • Account identifiers authorized by the user
  • Board, profile, or publishing destination information
  • Content metadata such as Pin titles, descriptions, destination links, media references, and publishing status
  • Basic usage and performance information related to published content

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Connect authorized accounts
  • Prepare, manage, and publish approved content
  • Organize content workflows
  • Provide reporting and performance insights
  • Maintain security, reliability, and service quality

Pinterest Data

If a user connects a Pinterest account, Rossler may use Pinterest API access only to perform actions authorized by that user, such as reading board information, preparing Pins, publishing approved Pins, and retrieving content performance information.

Rossler does not use Pinterest data for unauthorized purposes.

Data Sharing

Rossler does not sell user data.

We may share limited information only when necessary to:

  • Operate the service
  • Connect with authorized third-party platforms
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect the security and integrity of the service

Data Storage and Security

Rossler uses reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect account data, access tokens, and content information.

Users should not share private credentials directly with Rossler or its agents. Third-party account access should be granted through official authorization methods whenever available.

User Control and Revoking Access

Users may revoke third-party platform access at any time through the connected platform's account settings.

For Pinterest, users can revoke Rossler's access through their Pinterest account settings or by contacting Rossler.

Data Retention

Rossler retains information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, or resolve operational issues.
